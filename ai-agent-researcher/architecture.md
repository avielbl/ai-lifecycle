# Capability: Architecture Design

## Overview
Designs the model architecture and experiment tracking setup based on the Research Thesis and EDA findings.

## Operating Instructions
1. **Inputs:** Read `docs/00_Research_Thesis.md` and `docs/eda/02_EDA_Report.md`.
2. **Design Strategy:** Determine the framework stack, model topology, loss functions, and optimizers.
3. **Tracking:** Choose and configure `wandb`, `mlflow`, or `clearml`.
4. **Output:** Generate `docs/architecture/03_Architecture.md`.
