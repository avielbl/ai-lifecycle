# Capability: Retrospective

## Overview
Captures session learnings into the team knowledge base to avoid rediscovery of failures.

## Operating Instructions
1. **Inquiry:** Ask the user what was learned, what failed unexpectedly, and what parameters were validated.
2. **Knowledge Entry:** Document "Failed Attempts" sections rigorously.
3. **Output:** Generate/Append to `docs/knowledge/RETRO_EXP_[ID].md`.
